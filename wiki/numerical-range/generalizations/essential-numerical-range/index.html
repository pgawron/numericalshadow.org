Essential numerical range

The essential numerical range W_e(T) was introduced by Stampfli and Williams in stampfli1968growth for a bounded linear operator T in a Hilbert space H as the closure of the numerical range of the image of T in the Calkin algebra. Various equivalent characterizations were established, for example, in fillmore1972essential, along with applicatations, for example, in bogli2019essential.
